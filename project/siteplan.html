Site Plan

Site Name:

FlavorBites – A Delightful Online Food Store

Site Purpose

FlavorBites is an interactive website designed to provide customers with an easy way to browse and order a variety of delicious food products online. The site will offer an engaging user experience with a dynamic menu, special offers, and an integrated ordering system. The purpose of this website is to give an accessible way to order food and learn how to make some of them.

Scenarios

  • How can I view the complete menu of available meals and snacks?
  • What special offers are currently available?
  • Can I save my previous orders or favorite products for quick future purchases?
  • How can I contact support if I have questions or issues with my order?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: Deep Red (#D32F2F) - For headers and main buttons.
  • Secondary Color: Mustard Yellow (#FBC02D) - For promotions and featured offers.
  • Background Color: White (#FFFFFF) - To maintain clarity and professionalism.
  • Text Color: Dark Gray (#424242) - For the main content.
  • Accent Color: Lime Green (#388E3C) - For positive action buttons (like “Add to Cart”).

Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat Bold - Modern and professional style.
  • Body Text: Lato Regular - Ensures readability and elegance.
  • Buttons and Highlights: Raleway Medium - To draw attention to interactive elements.
